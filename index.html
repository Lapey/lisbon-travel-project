Why Lagos

Lagos, the city that never sleeps

The Lagos nightlife scene is truly one for the ages as the coastal town has a distinct charm and it’s easy to immerse yourself in the relaxed pace. But look further and you’ll discover there’s more to it, with lots of venues hosting wild parties for all crowds. Posh beach clubs, lively dive bars and intense dance clubs entertain locals and tourists well into the night.

Enjoy delicious grub, refreshing cocktails, watch sports and boogie to your heart’s content when roaming the cobbled streets. Many popular bars, clubs and discos are within easy walking distance of each other and you’ll easily find the scene for you. Chill out, join a raging party or mingle with the friendly locals of Lagos at their favourite hangouts. Here’s what to do at night in Lagos to round up your days in this fascinating Algarve town.
